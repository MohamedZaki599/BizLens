# Product Requirements Document (PRD): BizLens

## 1. Product Overview

**What BizLens Is**
BizLens is a streamlined, specialized SaaS financial dashboard designed specifically for small business owners, freelancers, and service providers. It acts as a financial command center, translating raw transactions into instant clarity.

**The Core Problem**
Many independent professionals and small businesses struggle to understand their true financial health. Traditional accounting software is overly complex, cluttered with unnecessary features, and requires specialized knowledge. Consequently, users are often disconnected from their cash flow and profitability.

**Why This Matters**
Financial clarity is the lifeblood of any business. When business owners spend hours deciphering spreadsheets or complex ERPs, they lose valuable time that could be spent growing their business. BizLens empowers them to understand their financial state in under 10 seconds, enabling faster, more confident decision-making.

---

## 2. Target Users

BizLens is tailored for users who need clarity without the overhead of enterprise accounting:

*   **Freelancers:** Independent contractors needing to separate personal and business expenses, track invoice payments, and monitor monthly income trends.
*   **Small Business Owners:** Founders and operators of boutique shops or small e-commerce stores who need to monitor gross margins and operating expenses at a glance.
*   **Service Providers:** Agencies, consultants, and local service businesses tracking project profitability and recurring client revenue.

---

## 3. User Pain Points

BizLens directly addresses the following critical pain points:

*   **Not understanding profit vs. expenses:** Users often see money in the bank but don't know how much is actual profit versus upcoming liabilities or overhead.
*   **Scattered financial tracking:** Relying on a messy combination of bank statements, Excel spreadsheets, and physical receipts.
*   **No actionable insights:** Existing tools provide raw data but don't tell the user *what it means* or *what to do next*.

---

## 4. Solution

BizLens solves these pain points by offering:

*   **Simple Dashboard:** A clean, noise-free interface that instantly surfaces net profit, total income, and total expenses.
*   **Insight Engine:** A smart, automated system that translates numbers into plain English (e.g., "Your expenses are up 15% this week," or "Design services are your most profitable category").
*   **Quick Transaction Tracking:** Frictionless entry for income and expenses, ensuring the system stays up-to-date with minimal effort.

---

## 5. Core Features

*   **Transactions (Income/Expense):**
    *   Global "Quick Add" capability accessible from anywhere in the app.
    *   Simple categorization, date logging, and amount tracking.
*   **Categories (Dynamic):**
    *   Customizable income and expense categories.
    *   Pre-seeded based on the user's specific "User Mode."
*   **Dashboard (Summary + Insights):**
    *   Visual breakdown of cash flow (Income vs. Expenses).
    *   **Insight Card:** Prominently placed at the top, delivering the most critical financial takeaway of the week/month.
*   **User Modes (Personalization):**
    *   *Freelancer*, *E-commerce*, or *Service Business*.
    *   Dynamically adjusts default categories, dashboard widget prioritization, and metric calculations to fit the specific business model.

---

## 6. Non-Goals

To maintain our focus on simplicity and clarity, BizLens will **explicitly not** include:

*   **Not a full accounting system:** We will not support double-entry bookkeeping, complex depreciation schedules, or strict tax compliance filing.
*   **Not an ERP:** We will not include inventory management, complex payroll systems, or supply chain logistics.

---

## 7. Success Metrics

We will measure the success of BizLens through the following KPIs:

*   **Time to Value (TTV):** The user understands their financial state in **< 10 seconds** upon opening the dashboard.
*   **Engagement:** High Daily Active Users (DAU) or Weekly Active Users (WAU), demonstrating the app is a trusted daily companion.
*   **Adoption:** The average number of transactions logged per user per week (indicating frictionless data entry).

---

## 8. Future Vision

As the product matures, we envision expanding BizLens to include:

*   **Multi-currency Support:** For freelancers and e-commerce stores dealing with international clients.
*   **Advanced Insights:** Predictive cash flow forecasting ("You may run short on cash for rent next week") using simple machine learning models.
*   **Reports:** One-click generation of clean, professional PDF summaries for investors, partners, or end-of-year tax prep.
